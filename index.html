Causes of the Civil War

One of the main causes of the Civil War was because of uncompromising differences between the free 
and slave states over the power of the national government to prohibit slavery in territories that 
had yet become states. When Abraham Lincol was elected in 1860, pledging to keep slavery out of 7 
territories, 7 slave states in the South seceded and formed a new nation, which the North didn't 
agree with.
